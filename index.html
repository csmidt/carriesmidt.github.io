About Me

This is me (in the driver's seat, of course!) with my three amazing kiddos!

According to an old English proverb, “necessity is the mother of invention”. For me necessity has been the mother of re-invention.
I have had to re-invent my professional self several times resulting in quite a varied background;
I have owned a race shop, was a franchisee, have been in insurance in several capacities,
have worked for a SasS company and most recently have become a Junior Front-End Engineer.

While these roles span multiple verticles, there are threads of commonality woven through all of them:
Vision, teamwork, communication, passion, prioritization, time management and discipline to name a few.
I am fortunate in that all of these experiences have taught me what I want and what I don’t want professionally.
I am very excited to be working in development and looking forward to building my portfolio!
